Which WWE Star Officiated Sasha Banks Wedding?

, The Ascension‘s Konnor recently officiated Sasha Banks nuptials.

Sources close to the wrestler tell The Sheet Konnor jumped at the chance to help Sasha, who is like a sister to him, and was honored when the couple asked if he’d preside over the ceremony.

The wedding between Banks and Sarath Ton — who wrestled on the indie scene as Kid Mikaze and now works for WWE as a costume designer — took place earlier this month.
