Leah Remini: What's In My Bag?

“I carry Lysol and Clorox wipes,” says Leah Remini, the star of TLC’s It’s All Relative (which returns July 15, 9 p.m.). “Every single person makes fun of me, but God forbid I find myself in a random hotel and need to wipe down the light switches, toilet, and remote!” The mom of Sofia, 11 (with husband Angelo Pagán), does a clean sweep of her Stella McCartney purse.

Shopping Assist

“My husband gave me my Louis Vuitton wallet. Well, I picked it out. I always tell him, ‘Don’t ever make a decision on your own!’”
