Is Kendall Jenner Following Sister Kim Kardashians Path?

For a lot of younger siblings, their big brothers and/or sisters are people who they’ve always looked up to.  In a lot of cases, they follow the example set by their siblings, and in many ways, try to emulate their success.  Unfortunately; however, all paths to success are not good to follow, and young Kendall Jenner may be on the road to finding that out. Recently, the 19 year-old model appeared in a photo shoot for the May issue of GQ Magazine, wearing nothing but a white string bikini.  While the photos do appear to have been done in a tasteful manner, many have been critical of the teenagers decision to participate in the shoot.  After all, she is the younger sister of Kim Kardashian, whose real claim to fame – besides being married to Kanye West – is the fact that she was the star of leaked sex tape.

In fact, many people seem to be critical of the entire Kardashian-Jenner family, with some stating that the whole group is nothing more than money hungry attention seekers.  However, some of these comments might be a little harsh.  Sure, Kendall is a little young, but she isn’t the first young model to appear in a photo shoot showing some skin, and she also seems to be taking her career as both a model and a business woman very seriously.  For the most part actually,  Kendall seems to be one of the most tame family members.  Unlike her younger sister, Kylie, Kendall seems to be completely focused on her career, and doesn’t typically make headlines for gossip related news.  However, the fact that she is a member of such a famous family will make it almost impossible for people to dissociate Kendall with the behavior of her sisters.  So far; however, she seems to be handling all the comments with poise.

What do you think; were these photos nothing more than tasteful model work, or does Kendall seem to be following down her sister, Kim Kardashian’s, path?
