Taylor Swift is having quite the summer and fall of 2023. While she is still on top of the music world thanks to her 2023 Eras tour, her possible relationship with NFL star Travis Kelce is so exciting and juicy that fans can't stop thinking about it.

Thankfully for good friends Travis Kelce and Patrick Mahomes, that doesn't seem to have happened. When Travis was dating Kayla Nicole, she and Brittany Mahomes became as tight as Travis and Patrick.

Did Taylor Swift Change Brittany Mahomes And Kayla Nicole's Friendship?

Brittany Mahomes and Kayla Nicole haven't commented on their friendship or said that they are no longer speaking. However, it does seem that their friendship has been affected by the fact that Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce could be dating. It's always heartbreaking when a new partner changes an existing friendship, and that could be the case here.

According to The Daily Mail, Kayla Nicole stopped following Brittany Mahomes. She went through her Instagram and decided to stop following Brittany's brother Jackson Mahomes and husband Patrick Mahomes as well.
