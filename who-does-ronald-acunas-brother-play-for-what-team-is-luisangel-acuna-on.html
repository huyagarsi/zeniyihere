Who does Ronald Acuas brother play for? What team is Luisangel Acua on?

Ronald José Acuña Blanco Jr. is a Venezuelan professional baseball outfielder currently plying his trade with the Atlanta Braves of Major League Baseball (MLB).

Ronald made his MLB debut in 2018, and won the National League Rookie of the Year Award.

Ronald was born into a family in love with baseball as his grandfather, Romualdo Blanco, and father, Ronald Acuña Sr., both played minor league baseball.

Ronald is the eldest of four sons and has a sibling, Luisangel Acuna who also plays baseball.

Who does Ronald Acuña’s brother play for?

Ronald Acuna’s brother Luisangel plays as a shortstop in the Texas Rangers organization.

What team is Luisangel Acuña on?

As mentioned earlier, Luisangel is on the Texas Rangers organization.

Luis angel answered previous questions about his ability to stay at shortstop by improving significantly in 2021 and earning organization defensive player of the year honors, though the free-agent signings of Corey Seager and Marcus Semien may move him elsewhere.

With his quickness and strong arm, he’s capable of playing almost anywhere on the diamond, with second base and center field his most obvious destinations.
