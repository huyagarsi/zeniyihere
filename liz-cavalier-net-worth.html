What is Liz Cavalier's Net Worth?

Liz Cavalier is an American alligator hunter and reality television personality who has a net worth of $300 thousand. Liz Cavalier joined the cast of the History Channel series, "Swamp People" during the show's second season. She was hired to work with fellow cast member Troy Landry, and quickly proved to be quite successful, earning the nickname, "Lucky Liz". Since Season 3, she has been based in Pecan Island, Louisiana, where she now has her own alligator hunting business.

Her partner is Kristi Broussard, and they are the only all-female alligator hunting team in operation. "Swamp People" is currently in its fourth season on the History Channel. When it premiered in 2010, the show set a ratings record for the network, and has remained consistently popular since then. It also spawned a spin-off show called, "Outback Hunters", and two video games, one arcade style, and one a hunting simulation.
