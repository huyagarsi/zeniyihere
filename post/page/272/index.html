A. Jon “The Barber” Gutwillig (born April 1, 1974) is the creator and guitarist of The Disco Biscuits, a trance fusion jam band from New York City. Since his participation in the formation of the biscuits in 1995, Jon and bassist Marc Brownstein have mostly shared songwriting responsibilities. Jon wrote the rock opera Hot Air Balloon, which premiered in 1998. According to folklore, his nickname “the barber” stems from his undergraduate days at the University of Pennsylvania, when he would take party attendees and place them in a barber chair while feeding them beverages and entertaining them.
