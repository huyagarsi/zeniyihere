Coco Austin Is Waist Training, Taking Care of Baby Chanel in Real Life Candid Pic

Supermom to the rescue! Coco Austin showed off her multitasking skills in an impressive selfie she shared via Instagram on Thursday, February 4. 

In the snapshot, the 36-year-old Ice Loves Coco star simultaneously waist-trains and snaps a photo in the bathroom mirror while toting her baby daughter, Chanel, with her free arm.

“Real life candid pic … Training and watching my baby simultaneously … The Coco way,” she captioned the picture. “Loving motherhood @waistedbykeke #ad.”

Austin, who welcomed Chanel last November, has gotten plenty of flack about her constant social media sharing and parenting skills from concerned fans.

“Coco darling, don’t exploit your newborn beautiful lil angel,” one Facebook user wrote on the deisgner’s Facebook wall in December. “I say with respect honey.”

“How is this exploiting her?” the model sniped back. “I’m proud that I’m finally a mama … does she not look happy?”

In the weeks following Chanel’s birth, Austin opened up to Us about all the naysayers who doubted she was even pregnant at all.

“Not every woman has the same pregnancy,” she told Us at the time. “I don’t really know what to say other than I have nothing to prove, and she’s here now.”

And later on in the month, the frustrated mother took to Twitter to air some more of her grievances.
