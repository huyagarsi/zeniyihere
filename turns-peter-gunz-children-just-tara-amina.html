Turns out Peter Gunz Has Children with More than Just Tara and Amina

Peter Gunz is one of the most entertaining characters on VH1’s reality TV series, Love & Hip Hop: New York.  During each episode, Gunz seems to find himself in the middle of a dramatic situation with one – or both – of baby’s mothers.  As a result of his behavior, Gunz often comes across as a lying womanizer who will say and do whatever it takes to make himself look good. However, despite his often ridiculous actions, many viewers are drawn to Gunz because they believe that he really is a nice guy underneath it all.  Although he has yet to reveal his good side, there is much more to Peter Gunz than most people think. Here are five things you didn’t know about Peter Gunz.

5. Peter Gunz is the father of rapper Corey Gunz.

Peter Gunz’ son, rapper Corey Gunz, gained mainstream recognition in 2011 when he was featured on Lil Wayne’s hit single, “6 foot 7 foot.” In 2011, Corey Gunz was featured on a reality TV series, Son of a Gun which also starred hit father.

4. Peter Gunz is not rich.

Peter Gunz may be a cast member on a hit reality show, but his pockets certainly don’t reflect it.  According to sources, Gunz’ net worth is only $50,000.  Several years ago, Gunz even admitted to being broke, stating that he was three month’s behind on his rent when he was called to be a part of Love & Hip Hop.

3. Peter Gunz had a hit single in 1998.

In 1998, Peter Gunz and Lord Tariq released a single titled, “Deja Vu (Uptown Baby).”  The single became a hit and has since been certified platinum in the United States.

2. Peter Gunz Twitter is like a diary.

Like most celebrities, Peter Gunz uses Twitter to keep his followers up-to-date on his latest moves.  However, Gunz also uses his Twitter account like a diary in which he documents all of his thoughts and feelings.

Contrary to what people think I'm not proud of my behavior I'm not proud of hurting anyone… Underneath the drama is love

— Peter Gunz (@IamPeterGunz) February 24, 2015

1. Tara and Amina aren’t the only women Peter Gunz has children with.

Peter Gunz relationship with both Tara and Amina are well documented in his storyline on Love & Hip Hop.  However, Tara and Amina aren’t the only women Peter Gunz has children with. According to sources, Gunz has a total of eight children and six baby mothers.
