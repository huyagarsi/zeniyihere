Jenelle Evans Shares Pictures of Revenge Body After Split

Look what you’re missing! Jenelle Evans is eating up the single life, posting pictures of her slim, fit, new revenge body on social media following her split from her fiancé, Nathan Griffith. She revealed her breakup earlier this month.

On Aug. 9, the Teen Mom 2 star snapped a racy selfie that showed off her cleavage in a black crop top and pink booty shorts. The next day, the MTV veteran, 23, posted a raunchy bikini shot, sunning herself on the beach.
