AGN President Debunks Death Rumors Surrounding Zack Orji

Emeka Rollas, the head honcho over at the Actors’ Guild of Nigeria (AGN), took to Instagram with a clear message: Zack Orji is alive and kicking. Yep, you heard it right – those rumors about him passing away are nothing but hot air.

Rollas didn’t hold back, calling out the troublemakers who get a kick for spreading fake news. He didn’t mince words, slamming their antics as downright wicked and unacceptable. And you know what? He’s spot on. Spreading rumors like that isn’t just mean; it’s downright cruel.

Imagine waking up to rumors of your own demise! It’s the kind of thing that makes you wonder what’s wrong with people. But Rollas wasn’t having any of it. He wanted to set the record straight, loud and clear: Zack Orji is here to stay, folks.

It’s a relief for fans who were worried sick after hearing the false reports. And it’s a reminder for all of us to think twice before hitting that share button. Fake news can do real damage, especially when it messes with people’s emotions like this.

So, let’s take a leaf out of Rollas‘ book and stick to the facts. And as for Zack Orji? Well, let’s just say he’s got plenty more good works to declare in the years to come – no fake news can change that.
