NJPW Satsuma no Kuni Results (4/29): 2023 KOPW Provisional TitleChanges Hands

On April 29, New Japan Pro-Wrestling held its NJPW Satsuma no Kuni event from Kagoshima Nishihara Shokai Arena in Kagoshima, Japan.

The event was headlined by two title matches; Aussie Open defended the IWGP Tag Team Championship against Shane Haste and Mikey Nicholls of TMDK, and Taichi challenged Shingo Takagi for the KOPW 2023 Provisional Championship.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Bishamon (Hirooki Goto & YOSHI-HASHI) def. Toru Yano & Boltin Oleg
  • HOUSE OF TORTURE (Yujiro Takahashi & EVIL) def. Shota Umino & Oskar Leube
  • United Empire (TJP, Francesco Akira & Aaron Henare) def. Kevin Knight, KUSHIDA & Tomoaki Honma
  • United Empire (Jeff Cobb & Great-O-Khan) def. TMDK (Zack Sabre Jr. & Kosei Fujita)
  • Minoru Suzuki, Ren Narita, Yuto Nakashima & El Desperado def. Kazuchika Okada, Tomohiro Ishii, Ryohei Oiwa & Togi Makabe
  • Hikuleo, Tama Tonga, Jado & Master Wato def. BULLET CLUB (KENTA, David Finlay, Gedo & Taiji Ishimori
  • Just 5 Guys (SANADA, DOUKI & Yoshinobu Kanemaru) def. Los Ingobernables De Japon (Tetsuya Naito, BUSHI & Hiromu Takahashi)
  • Aussie Open (Kyle Fletcher & Mark Davis) (c) def. TMDK ( Mikey Nicholls & Shane Haste)
  • EVIL and Yujiro make it clear that they intend to challenge for IWGP Tag Team Titles.
  • Taichi def. Shingo Takagi (c) 3-2 to win the championship.
