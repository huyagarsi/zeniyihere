Niecy Nash Ex-Husband: Meet Jay Tucker

Actress, comedian, and television host Niecy Nash is American. On February 23, 1970, in Palmdale, California, Carol Denise Ensley was born.

The television shows “Reno 911!” and “The Soul Man,” as well as her work as a host and producer on the TLC series “Clean House,” are what Nash is best known for.

Additionally, she has acted in a variety of movies, such as “Love, Wedding, Marriage,” “Code Name: The Cleaner,” and “Soul Plane.”

For her roles in the Netflix series “When They See Us” and the HBO series “Getting On,” Nash has recently won praise from the critics.

For her work in television and movies, she has won and been nominated for many honors, including an Emmy.

Niecy Nash Ex-Husband: Meet Jay Tucker

She announced her engagement to Jay Tucker, an electrical engineer, in 2010.

According to People, their lavish wedding took place in Malibu in May 2011 and was covered in a two-hour program by TLC.

They had been dating for eight years and were smitten with each other.

Niecy Nash and Jay Tucker announced their divorce in October 2019.

“In this season of our lives, we are better friends than marital partners,” they wrote at the time, announcing the news via an Instagram post.
