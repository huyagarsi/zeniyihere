Zineb Triki (born 1980) is a French actress of Moroccan descent, known for her role as Nadia El Mansour in the French television series The Bureau.

Biography

Zineb Triki was born in 1980 in Morocco. She attended a French school, where she took up theatre and classical dance. She moved to Paris at the age of 15 to attend High School.

She obtained a degree in political science in the university of McGill in Canada, and worked briefly as a journalist. She obtained a master’s degree in political science from the university La Sorbonne in Paris, then obtained another master’s degree in audio-visual production in Paris.

Triki began her acting career in earnest from 2009, with short roles in 14h05 and The Misadventures of Franck and Martha. Her first film roles were in Deux fenêtres in 2013 and La Marche verse (2016). It was the TV series The Bureau that brought her to prominence with her role of Nadia El Mansour.

Her performance as Nassim’s mother in the 2017 film De toutes mes forces was described as luminous, in this short and difficult role.
